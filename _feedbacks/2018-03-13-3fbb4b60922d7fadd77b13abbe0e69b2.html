Hi Ben, Eira, and Whitney,

The SAA Preservation Section is organizing a Twitter Conference for Preservation Week 2018, and I am wondering if some portion of your team would be interested and able to participate on April 26th? I think that the preservation community could benefit from hearing about your project.
https://www2.archivists.org/groups/preservation-section/preservation-week-2018-twitter-conference

The idea is to provide a free and inclusive forum to share short "presentations" on topics related to preservation in libraries and archives. Each speaker would be assigned a session of 15 minutes to share 6-15 tweets/slides on a topic of their choosing. As one of the goals is to share useful knowledge, the talk doesn't have to be original or groundbreaking research. We hope that by clustering a number of sessions together that we can create some momentum to reach a broad audience with a range of topics, whether practical, project based, or theoretical.

If you are interested, just let me know and fill out this brief proposal form: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdUwNyW2rxpcEIPv5GspmYdvKgkFlmyeD4o8orGbuWoVQRi4w/viewform?usp=sf_link

Many thanks,
Fletcher
